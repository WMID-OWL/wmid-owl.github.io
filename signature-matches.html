“…ALL’S FAIR…”

Love and War

A 5-on-5 elimination match held at All Love Lost. Winners are randomly placed within the final 10 Overthrow Rumble entry spots. Losers are randomly placed within the first 10 entry spots.

30 ENTER, 4 REMAIN, 1 WITH THE OPPORTUNITY TO OVERTHROW IT ALL.

Overthrow Rumble

A 30-person battle royal where the match changes at the final four. Once four competitors remain, over-the-top eliminations stop counting and the match becomes an elimination-style Fatal Four-Way.

LAST CHANCE. NO DOORS. NO ESCAPE.

Hex-Cell Eliminator

Six qualifiers enter the cell at No Escape. Two begin, four enter one by one, and eliminations happen by pinfall, submission, or KO until one final title challenger survives.

GOLD, GLORY, OR GOODBYE.

Fate’s Wheel

An 8-person elimination chaos match held at Survivor’s Remorse. The winner earns a title shot, while the next five finishers draw sealed cases that may contain opportunity or Remorse.

POWER ALWAYS COMES DUE.

The Devil’s Contract

A 6-person no-DQ elimination match where the winner earns a cash-in contract. If the cash-in fails, the Debt Clause burns the contract and punishes the failed holder.

NO HYPE. NO SHORTCUTS. JUST PROOF.

The Proving Ground

A four-week round-robin tournament where selected wrestlers earn Combat Points before the top two meet at the Proving Grounds PPV final.

NO RING. NO RULES. NO MERCY.

IBYA

Short for “I’ll Beat Your Ass,” IBYA is OWL’s rare underground fight-club match. Competitors fight inside a 12-sided cage, and victory can only happen by KO or submission.
